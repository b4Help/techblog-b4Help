# Hello World: Why I Started This Blog

Every dev blog starts somewhere. This is mine.

## The why

I've been meaning to document my work for a while — not because anyone asked, but because writing forces clarity. If I can't explain something simply, I probably don't understand it well enough yet.

## What's coming

Expect posts on:

- **Tools I use** — CLI tools, editors, extensions worth knowing
- **Code walkthroughs** — real problems, real solutions
- **Things I broke and fixed** — the best kind of learning

## Stack for this blog

This blog runs on [Jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com/) with the [Midnight theme](https://github.com/pages-themes/midnight), hosted for free on [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/).

Zero cost. Zero fuss. Just Markdown and git.
